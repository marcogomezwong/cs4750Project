About REFUGE

What is HooPoo?

HooPoo has a target audience of students, faculty, and staff at the University of Virginia. HooPoo would be used in many different instances, one main one being when students are looking for the nearest, or cleanest, bathroom. Often students, especially incoming first years, are unaware of where the bathrooms at the University are, particularly in the buildings where the men and women bathrooms are not nearby each other. HooPoo will not only assist users in finding a restroom, but also offer information about its cleanliness, general rating, and reviews from previous users. Another important use of HooPoo is for custodians, as they will get alerted of immediate needs from each bathroom and of poor ratings and reviews. This makes it easier for them to do their job efficiently and effectively, as they cannot constantly check each bathroom for supply levels and current state.
